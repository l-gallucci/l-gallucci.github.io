About Me

I'm a microbial ecologist fascinated by how life thrives in extreme environments — particularly deep-sea hydrothermal vents. My research integrates omics, chemistry, and modeling to understand how microbes influence the cycling of iron, sulfur, and trace elements in hydrothermal plumes.

Beyond research, I enjoy coding, designing games, and cooking — exploring creativity across both science and art.
